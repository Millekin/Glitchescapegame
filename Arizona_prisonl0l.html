Inmate activities for the day.

We will first open their cells at 5.30am, one by one and make them take a shower for 5 minutes, then we will take them all in the hall for breakfast until 7am. They will then run around the complex grounds for five laps to keep fit. All of that strictly watched by the security guards, of course. They will then have access to the library for 5 hours. After that, they will have free time until 7o'clock in the evening, they will eat dinner and lights out at 8.30pm.
